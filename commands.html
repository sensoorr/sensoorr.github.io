Commands

Our commands are:

  • .help | Sends all of the commands
  • .play [song name / URL] | Plays the specified song
  • .pause | Pauses the song
  • .resume | Resumes the audio
  • .ping | Sends the current ping
  • .uptime | Sends the bots uptime
  • .queue | Sends the queue
  • .nowplaying | Sends what is currently playing
  • .save | Sends a DM with the song info
  • .stop | Stops the song, removes the queue and leaves
  • .search [song name] | Searches for the song
  • .skip | Skips the current playing song
  • .progress | Sends the current progress through the song
  • .clear | Clears the queue
  • .statistics | Shows the bots statistics
  • .loop [mode] | Loops the song with the specified mode
  • .filter [bassboost, 8D, nightcore] | Applies the specified filter to the song
